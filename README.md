# AutoMind 🚗

**Live Demo:** [https://auto-mind-hmlo.vercel.app/dashboard](https://auto-mind-hmlo.vercel.app/dashboard)

AutoMind is a product concept and working prototype designed to improve transparency in car ownership.

The project focuses on a common problem: most drivers do not have a clear, organized, and trustworthy way to manage service history, understand repair issues, compare garages, or estimate the real value of their car.

## Demo Overview

The demo presents AutoMind as a digital layer around the car ownership journey, combining maintenance visibility, AI-assisted support, and service decision tools.

### Dashboard
The dashboard gives a high-level overview of the vehicle, including:
- current car details
- ownership and maintenance summary
- market value visibility
- quick access to core product actions

### Service History
This flow presents the idea of a digital service book.
Users can review past maintenance activity and explore the concept of scanning receipts into structured service records.

### AI Mechanic
This page demonstrates an AI-assisted support flow for warning lights and car issues.
The purpose is to help users better understand potential problems before going to a garage.

### Find Garage / Marketplace
This area demonstrates how users can search for nearby garages, compare service options, and explore a more transparent decision flow.

### Share / Vehicle History Export
The product also includes the concept of sharing vehicle history in a cleaner and more trustworthy way for future buyers.

## What the Demo Is Meant to Show

This prototype is designed to demonstrate:
- product thinking around trust and transparency
- end-to-end user flow design
- MVP scoping
- AI-driven product concepts
- a broader vision for scaling the product beyond the initial demo

Some parts of the experience are presented as prototype flows or simulated interactions, while the main goal is to communicate the product direction clearly.

## Why I Built This

I built AutoMind as a Product Management portfolio project.

The goal was to explore a real-world consumer problem, define the user pain points clearly, and design a product that combines:
- practical user value
- AI-enabled workflows
- clear product structure
- future scalability
